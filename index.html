Description

Hashcube is a cross platform mobile and social gaming platform with multiple titles on iOS, Android, Amazon and Facebook platforms. Our flagship title, Sudoku Quest is the most monetizing Sudoku app across platforms, and has monetized 10x more than competition. With over 3 million users, the game is available across Android, iOS, Amazon and Facebook platforms and has been consistently growing over the years in terms of user base and revenue. Our latest title, Gummy Pop is a one of a kind ‘Chain reaction’ game that has the potential to disrupt the monopoly of match- 3 genre with its uniquely exciting and engaging mechanism. With 280 unique levels, 5 different variations and 9 exciting power-ups to play with, Gummy pop surely has the potential to reach the top grossing charts globally.

History

History

Hashcube, started its journey back in 2008, with a sudoku website and has grown to be one of the few studios in India building successful casual games on freemium model. When our co-founders started they just wanted to make games that are simple and fun to play. The ‘simplicity’ has always been the key ingredient of our games here at Hashcube. Our co-founders believed “Simple can be harder than complex”, and that approach has driven the team to make easy to play, fun games for our users.
