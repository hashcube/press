Description

STOP MATCHING and START POPPING! Pop the adorable GUMMIES to create incredible Chain Reactions! Download Now and enjoy the crazy chain reactions, super-fun power-ups, amazing level variations and incredible popping fun across hundreds of poptastic levels!

Features

  • Unique and Exciting Chain Reaction Mechanic: An interesting new take on chain reaction mechanic: Grow Gummies - Pop Gummies - Create amazing chain reactions.
  • Amazing Power Ups to help you in sticky situations: 9 awesome powerups to choose from, select the powerup that suits your gameplay style.
  • Adorable Characters that you'll love: Four different gummies that grow from the sleepy Green gummy to the active Red gummy.
  • 5 distinct game variations: Never run out of fun with 5 different game variations. Keep an eye out for the interesting challenges.
  • Deceptively Simple and Incredibly Fun Gameplay : Strategize to form clusters of red gummies, the bigger the clusters, the better the Chain reactions
  • Captivating Music and Sound: Enjoy your journey across the mystery lands with awesome music to keep you entertained.
  • Compete with friends for high scores : Challenge your friends, beat them by saving more gummies, compete on global leaderboards and be the Poppin’ champ.
  • Play across multiple devices without losing progress: Sync with facebook and save your progress across all platforms, including Android, iOS and Amazon.

About Hashcube

Hashcube is a cross platform mobile and social gaming platform with multiple titles on iOS, Android, Amazon and Facebook platforms. Our flagship title, Sudoku Quest is the most monetizing Sudoku app across platforms, and has monetized 10x more than competition. With over 3 million users, the game is available across Android, iOS, Amazon and Facebook platforms and has been consistently growing over the years in terms of user base and revenue. Our latest title, Gummy Pop is a one of a kind ‘Chain reaction’ game that has the potential to disrupt the monopoly of match- 3 genre with its uniquely exciting and engaging mechanism. With 280 unique levels, 5 different variations and 9 exciting power-ups to play with, Gummy pop surely has the potential to reach the top grossing charts globally.
